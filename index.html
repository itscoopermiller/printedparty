Staff Training

Packaging

  • Weighing
    1. Choose grams on scale
    1. Count 75 of heavier product, 100 of lighter product. The more product the more accurate the count is.
    1. Lay product on scale and hold the PCS button
    1. Press M to select the correct number of products on scale
    1. Press PCS again to lock in the number

  • Product Checking
    • Fan through each page (all products including wine labels)
    • Check colors, paper stock, for streaks, quality
    • Confirm you have the correct cover sheet. You can verify this by checking the SKU on your time sheet against the SKU on the cover sheet.
    • If packaging wine labels, fan through wine labels to make sure it includes the correct pages.
  • Standards & other tips
    • Rubber band wine labels in stacks of 25
    • Some products we receive will include overages from our printer. Package as many sets as you can with the supplies you have.
    • Our piece rates are cushioned so that you have time to check products. Our customers are ruthless. Mistakes like a shorted set or damaged product being packaged could result in a bad review that could destroy an entire listing.
    • If you have damaged product, send photos to Cooper for further instructions.

Pick Up (Thursdays)

  • All of your work will be labeled on a designated set of shelves for pick-up. This includes cover sheets.
  • Open up your app the the "Pick-Up" page for a shopping list of everything you need to pack for the week.
  • If picking up multiple bag sizes, mark the bag number on the outside so that you know where to return more easily

Drop Off (Thursdays)

  • Return products to the drop off shelf
  • On the outside of the box include the following in sharpie:
    • SKU
    • Quantity of finished product
    • Box Size
    • Box Weight
  • Box, package and tape any full cases of products.
  • Return all unused bags to the correct locations (a ruler is hanging on the shelf to help.)
  • Return extra product to correct boxes in the "Extras" are
  • If you have any product shortages, notify Cooper.
  • Submit your schedule on the app

Working in the warehouse

  • You are welcome to schedule time to work from the warehouse if you would like.
    • Kids are permitted in the warehouse when you are the only employee at that time.
    • Please keep food in the break room, or if you eat at the work tables, please wipe them down afterwards.
    • Break down boxes when you leave and try to fill the recycling bin vertically so they’re easier to transport to the dumpster.
    • Vacuum around your work area before you leave if needed.

Time Off

Product is ordered and schedules are made well in advance so we would really appreciate notice ASAP, but at least 3 weeks prior to your scheduled time off.
